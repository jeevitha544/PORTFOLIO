Driver Drowsiness Detection System🚘

Driver fatigue is a significant contributor to road accidents worldwide, often resulting in severe injuries and fatalities. To address this issue this project presents a real-time driver drowsiness detection system using computer vision techniques. The system leverages Python, OpenCV and dlib libraries to monitor a driver’s eye and mouth movements through a webcam feed.Key facial landmarks are extracted to calculate the Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) and Mouth Aspect Ratio (MAR) which are used to detect prolonged eye closure and yawning- key indicators of drowsiness.. Upon detecting signs of drowsiness the system triggers immediate alerts using both audible alarms and email notifications via Mailgun thereby enhancing the likelihood of driver response.
